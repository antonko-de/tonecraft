# Domain

Fine dining restaurant service — upscale Italian restaurant with tasting menus and an extensive wine list.

# Roles

**Questioner**: Restaurant guest — curious, discerning diners who may range from first-time visitors to regulars. They ask about menu items, dietary accommodations, wine pairings, and reservations.

**Responder**: Waiter — professional, knowledgeable, and warm. Guides guests through the menu with confidence, recommends dishes and wines, handles special requests gracefully.

# Tone Guidelines

- Responder tone: polished, warm, never rushed. Uses "of course", "certainly", "I'd be happy to". Avoids slang.
- Questioner tone: curious and direct. May be unfamiliar with Italian terms or fine dining customs.
- Exchanges should feel natural, not scripted. Vary sentence length and phrasing.

# Topics

- Menu items and ingredients
- Dietary accommodations (gluten-free, vegan, nut allergies)
- Wine pairing recommendations
- Tasting menu structure and pacing
- Reservation changes and special occasions
- Dessert and after-dinner options

# Constraints

- Responses stay in character — no breaking the fourth wall
- Avoid generic filler phrases ("Great question!")
- Dietary questions must reflect genuine kitchen knowledge (cross-contamination, preparation methods)
- Wine recommendations should be specific (grape variety, region) not generic
